Themen dieses Kurses

  • Allgemeines

    Allgemeines

    • Welcome to the course on Introduction to Machine Learning!

      By the end of this course you will be able to understand and explain the concepts of machine learning, as well as understand the formalized concepts and methods of this vast field. You will also be able to implement these concepts and methods in the form of algorithms. Through active participation in this module, you will be best placed to sensibly select, adapt, and apply the methods you learned.

      Please refer to the course homepage for up-to-date information.
